Graffiti conjecture 194 (2 students)

This computer generated conjecture claims that:
every simple connected graph G on n ≥ 2 vertices satisfying α(G) ≤ 1 + λ_avg(v) has a Hamiltonian path.
Test the conjecture in various ways for small and big graphs. You can also use some metaheuristic
to test for larger graphs.
Some comments:
1.
α(G)is the independence number of a graph, i.e., the maximum number of vertices such that no
two are adjacent.
2.
λ(v)is the local independence of a vertex v, i.e.. the independence number of the subgraph induced
by the neighbors of vertex v.  The average of λ(v), also denoted λ_avg(v), is the average of all local
independence number of the vertices of the graph.
3.  
A graph has a Hamiltonian path if there exist two vertices with a path between them which visits
each vertex of the graph exactly once.

GC 194
Ta računalniško generirana konjektura trdi:
Vsak preprost, povezan graf G na n ≥ 2 vozliščih, ki zadošča α(G) ≤ 1 + λ_avg(v), ima hamiltonsko pot.

Komentarji:
1. α(G) je neodvistnostno število grafa, torej največje število vozlišč, od katerih nista nobeni sosednji.
2. λ(v) je lokalna neodvistnost grafa, torej α(G) podgrafa, ki ga določajo sosednja vozlišča vozlišča v. Z λ_avg(v) označimo povprečno λ(v) grafa G.
3. Graf ima hamiltonsko pot, če obstajata dve vozlišči, ki ju povezuje pot, ki natančno enkrat obišče vsako vozlišče grafa.
